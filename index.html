Who I Am

My name is Laurel Vaughn and I am a freelance and professional graphic designer, web designer, and photographer living in South Carolina. With a degree in visual communications and years of practical experience, I specialize in creating and managing content across a wide variety of media – from print to digital.

I work full-time as a Digital Media Manger, using my skills and experience to oversee and improve websites, digital ads and photographs, and mass email communications for a performing arts center in Greenville.

I am also available for freelance photography or design. For pricing, please contact me using the form below.
